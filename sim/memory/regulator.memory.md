The Authority has written to Amalgamated Widget Holdings plc for the third time concerning the trade in wadgets (ref. FCAGD/AWH/0142). This follows previous letters FCAGD/AWH/0123 (6th Jan) and FCAGD/AWH/0137 (15th March), to which no substantive response has been received. The current letter set a deadline of 17th July 2026 for a full schedule of wadgets, material composition, trading volumes, and the role of Widgetco (Innovations) Ltd. The Authority also enquired whether the company's wadgets are in fact gaskets, a separate regulated category. The Authority awaits a response. Separate concurrent interests remain in wickets (refs. 0114, 0132), fenestration (ref. 0119), non-return valves (ref. 0134), and the round ones (ref. 0128).
