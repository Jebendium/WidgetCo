The Authority has now written to Amalgamated Widget Holdings plc for the seventh time. The present letter (Ref FCAGD/AWH/0150, 23rd July 2026) concerns non-return valves, being the second letter on that topic (following Ref 0134 of 19th March 2025). The Authority has now written on six separate topics: wadgets (5 letters, Refs 0123, 0137, 0142, 0146, and a fifth whose reference is not immediately to hand), cricket wickets/stumps (3 letters, Refs 0114, 0145, 0148), non-return valves (2 letters, Refs 0134, 0150), fenestration (1 letter, Ref 0119), and the round ones (1 letter, Ref 0128). No substantive response has ever been received to any of these letters. The Authority notes the 20th August 2026 deadline and will follow up as necessary. The Authority remains confident in its jurisdictional remit, untested though it remains.
