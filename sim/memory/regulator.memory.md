The Authority has written to Amalgamated Widget Holdings plc concerning the manufacture and supply of non-return valves (ref. FCAGD/AWH/0134). This is a new and distinct matter, separate from our concurrent interests in wickets (ref. 0114, 0132), fenestration (ref. 0119), wadgets (ref. 0123), and the round ones (ref. 0128). We have required a schedule of valve products, confirmation of flow-direction marking, an explanation of Widgetco (Innovations) Ltd's role in the valve supply chain, and a physical sample for inspection. A response is expected within 28 days. The Authority remains watchful.
