The Authority has written to Amalgamated Widget Holdings plc concerning the trade in wadgets (ref. FCAGD/AWH/0137), following up on our prior letter (ref. FCAGD/AWH/0123) to which no substantive response has been received. We have now required a full schedule of wadgets, material composition details, trading volumes, and an explanation of Widgetco (Innovations) Ltd's role. A response is expected within 28 days. The Authority considers the wadget matter to be ongoing and unresolved. Separately, we retain concurrent but distinct interests in wickets (refs. 0114, 0132), fenestration (ref. 0119), non-return valves (ref. 0134), and the round ones (ref. 0128).
