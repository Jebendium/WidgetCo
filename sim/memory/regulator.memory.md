The Authority has written to Amalgamated Widget Holdings plc for the fourth time concerning the manufacture of cricket wickets, stumps and associated sporting apparatus (Ref FCAGD/AWH/0145). This follows previous correspondence on wickets (Refs FCAGD/AWH/0114 and FCAGD/AWH/0132), to which no substantive response has been received. The letter requires, within 28 days, a schedule of wicket types and quantities, proposed arrangements for bail marking, a declaration as to timber species and origin, and an explanation of Widgetco (Innovations) Ltd's role in the wicket supply chain. The Authority notes that separate concurrent interests remain in wadgets (Refs 0123, 0137, 0142 — deadline 17th July 2026 approaching), fenestration (Ref 0119), non-return valves (Ref 0134), and the round ones (Ref 0128).
